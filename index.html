Hitachi Elevator Asia offers high quality, technologically advanced and reliable forms of vertical and horizontal transportation, including elevators and escalators. We have expanded our business to include retrofitting of such equipment, among other urban living solutions.

Hitachi Elevator Asia offers high quality elevator systems that improve a building's serviceability and, therefore, play a crucial role in overall operations. Hitachi elevators are easy to install and customizable according to the specific requirements of buildings. The elevators are designed and built to be the most efficient, economical and reliable. Hitachi has developed a broad range for various types of use, including Passenger, Freight, Observation and Home models.
